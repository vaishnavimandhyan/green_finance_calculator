This tool is a financial calculator designed to show investors the tangible environmental impact of putting their money into a Green Bond instead of a standard corporate bond.

When a user enters their investment amount and the number of years they want to hold it, the program calculates two major metrics:

The Financial Return: It models the compounding interest over time based on the bond's fixed yield.
The Sustainability Impact: It calculates the exact mass of carbon dioxide emissions (CO_2) prevented because that money was specifically used to fund clean energy infrastructure.
